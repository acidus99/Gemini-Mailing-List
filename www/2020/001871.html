Everything monospace seems to be fit to the width of the viewport, which
might be good for ASCII-art, but not much for text with extreme line
lengths. I put together a temporary test page with both short and long
examples.

=> gemini://x-z.se/test.gemini
Might move or delete later, but I promise it will still be there at least a
few weeks from now.

I know next to nothing about Android app making but I feel like fit to
width is a reasonable approach for the first versions of the app. Having a
min and max text size will be a better user experience in the future.
