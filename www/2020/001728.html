I have completed most of what is necessary for a YouTube interface for Gemini!!!
It has a Search feature, Channel pages, Playlist pages, and Video pages. Video pages
will link to the YouTube url for that video.

You can view it here: gemini://pon.ix.tc:1965/cgi-bin/youtube.cgi

The next things I will be doing:
* Expose comments on their own pages so we can see video comments
* Experiment with the Certificate stuff so you can subscribe to YouTube Channels but
completely detached from the YouTube site's subs. I can then show recent videos from
your subs when using a certificate.
* Other useful video information like View Count, Like Count, Quality, Duration
* Related Videos List
* Direct video download?
