The main differences with what my server is doing are:

* I use CSV format
* I do not log the IP but its sha1 hash, because of privacy concerns
* I use ISO 8601 for date because it’s easy to output from PHP
* I do not log the body length, but I log the meta line instead, which is important (especially for errors)
