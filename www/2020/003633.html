Hi,

I'm still really excited about gemini and it's community and was eager to try out a few things. 
As a long time perl dude I started hacking a few cgi scripts, one of them i'd like to introduce now: 
orrg

orrg is a script that renders rss/atom feeds fetched over gemini or https as gemtext pages. 
It also includes a list of recently visited and most popular feeds on a particular instance.

My public instance is running at gemini://orrg.clttr.info

further ressources:

source: https://git.sr.ht/~rwa/orrg
mailing list: https://lists.sr.ht/~rwa/gmni-perl-cgi

Feedback welcome.
