It would be really helpful if you could mock up feeds for modules A, B, and
C, where A is joining, B & C are existing, and A attaches to B.

In particular, the feed content at the start, for A, B, and C, along with
artefacts produced during and after merge. This could be done dynamically
with actual code, or just visually with before/after file contents.

I think there's a lot of potential in the concept. Discovery is, in my
opinion, the biggest issue of the Internet, and it's been woefully
neglected (probably because algorithms stepped in and distracted us with
ruthless efficiency!)

While aggregators and search engines are good, they ultimately present us
with lists of stuff that's often only loosely related. This can be fun in
itself, but there's lots more we could innovate on.

Kevin
