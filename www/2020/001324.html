The robots exclusion standard defines a simple syntax to control access:

User-agent: *
Allow: /

Such syntax can express well-known locations:

Allow: /.well-known/feed --- syndication url
Allow: /.well-known/host-meta -- RFC6415
Allow: /.well-known/icon  -- favorite icon url

Data encoded in text/gemini. To advertise capabilities.
