Hey all!

I posted a link to a project I was working on back in July, Flounder.

https://flounder.online/
gemini://flounder.online/

I wanted to make something that makes it easy for users who may not have
familiarity with SSH/FTP/Linux to set up a simple Gemini capsule and
log, accessible over Gemini and HTTP. I've made a lot of progress
that I wanted to update you all on if you're interested. Aside from a
rewrite from Rust to Go and some UI/UX improvements, I also added
(experimental) WebDAV support and built-in support for the newly-added
companion Gemini Feed specification, e.g.:

gemini://alex.flounder.online/gemlog
https://alex.flounder.online/gemlog

Flounder is still under active development, and there are a lot of
improvements I want to make, such as adding support for custom domains,
but it's much more usable than it was last time I updated the mailing
list about it. Feel free to make an account -- in order to cut down on
spam, I have to manually approve signups, but I usually get to that
within a few hours to a day. 

If anyone is interested in hosting Flounder, either as a single-user
instance or for others, please let me know! It shouldn't be too hard to
self-host and I can help you with any questions you may have. I'd also
gladly appreciate any patches.

https://sr.ht/~alexwennerberg/flounder/

And of course, this is 100% free, open source, etc. 

All the best,

Alex
