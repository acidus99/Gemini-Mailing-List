The best thing about gemini is its markup language. I liked it so much that I decided to add its support to my wiki engine MycorrhizaWiki. Well, the support is not implemented by me, it is implemented by a fellow named DanInSpace104. Thanks!

I think Mycorrhiza's support of text/gemini may support other developers to support text/gemini in non-gemini things. Talking about non-geminity, Mycorrhiza supports web only for now, but I do not exclude the possibility that I will add a gemini front-end one day.

By the way, is text/gemini the only name the markup language has? What about geminitext? It's not really different but sounds cool and is highly usable. In fact, it's already being used in Mycorrhiza's dev chat!
