Hi there,

I just "finished" creating a gemini server to serve my own
geminilog on gemini://gsthnz.com

https://sr.ht/~gsthnz/satellite

It's passing the gemini-diagnostics test suite from michael-lazar on
github.

There's some improvements to be made still, one of them is the automatic
management of self-signed certificates. The idea is to be super simple
to use, only requiring a root directory to serve and a domain to work,
and everything else is managed for you.

I had A LOT of fun making this.

Hope you guys like it!
