Hi. I've written a simple and basic syntax-highlighting mode for
text/gemini for emacs, available at
https://git.carcosa.net/jmcbray/gemini.el. It doesn't support any
multiline constructs yet (e.g. pre blocks), and I'd like to highlight
the different elements of a link differently, which it doesn't do yet,
but it's good enough for basic use at this point. Pull requests from
people who are better at elisp than me are welcome.
