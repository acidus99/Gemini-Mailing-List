For exactly one year
<gemini://gemini.bortzmeyer.org/software/lupa/logbook.gmi>, the Lupa
crawler <gemini://gemini.bortzmeyer.org/software/lupa/> has been
crawling the geminispace to gather information and produce statistics
<gemini://gemini.bortzmeyer.org/software/lupa/stats.gmi>.

During this year, the geminispace seen by Lupa went from 50,000
working URLs to 334,000 and from 415 working capsules to 1,515. Thanks
to all the people who make capsules, read them and write software.
