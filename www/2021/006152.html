Hi everyone!

I just released comitium, yet another feed aggregator for Gemini!  None of the
other aggregators that I've found do quite everything I want them to do, so I
decided to write my own.

It supports Atom, RSS, JSON, and Gemini feeds, as well as watching pages for
changes.  It also supports feeds over Gemini, Gopher, and HTTP.  The source,
(hopefully) decent documentation, and a getting started guide are here:
https://git.nytpu.com/comitium/about/

You can see a running instance on my capsule here:
gemini://nytpu.com/feed.gmi
