Hi All,

As simple author I would say that I am not interested in having compatibility between Markdown and Gemini/Text.

I have a bias cause I decided to serve my thoughts exclusively on Gemini, however there are out there plenty of scripts that already convert from Markdown to Gemini/Text and most of them are very trivial to use.

The ones who provide content on both protocols already solved this issue, they write before in Markdown and convert in Gemini/text after.

Cheers,

TGL
