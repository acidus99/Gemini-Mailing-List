After some testing by my friends and talking to René. I'm excited to share my new search provider for the Gemini protocol, TLGS
gemini://tlgs.one

This a new search engine. It started as a weekend project to implement a Gemini server and client in C++. Then gradually grown out of control. And I had shamefully stole some page design and wordings from the awesome gemini://geminispace.info due to my limited English skills.

The codebase is dirty right now. But I'm happy that it works. I plan to update the index twice a week. I guess we'll see how it handles real traffic from Gemini.
