The protocol specification is, of course, the authoritative place for
the final word on these things.  But it's been pointed out in the past
that people really shouldn't need to read through the entire spec just
in order to know how to write a document, which is entirely true.  So,
behold!

gemini://gemini.circumlunar.space/docs/gemtext.gmi
gemini://gemini.circumlunar.space/docs/cheatsheet.gmi

Two new resources to help beginners to get up and writing quickly.
Feedback welcome!
