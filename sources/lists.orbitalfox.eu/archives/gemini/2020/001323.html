The tag URI  is defined as follow:

"tag:" authorityName "," YYYY-MM-DD-date ":" specific [ "#" fragment ]

text/gemini document could sport one, to convey authorship.

One could turn a gemini url into a tag, e.g.:

gemini://gemini.circumlunar.space/users/solderpunk/cornedbeef/microblogging-via-gemini-a-sketch.gmi
⇵
authorityName: gemini.circumlunar.space/users/solderpunk
date: 2020-06-01
specific: 5EFkbD
fragment: 16:59:06
⇵
tag:gemini.circumlunar.space/users/solderpunk,2020-06-01:5EFkbD#16:59:06


gemini://gemini.conman.org/gRFC/0002 
⇵
authorityName: gemini.conman.org
date: 2019-08-01
specific: 6tXsH
fragment: 20:46:12
⇵
tag:gemini.conman.org,2019-08-01:6tXsH#20:46:12
