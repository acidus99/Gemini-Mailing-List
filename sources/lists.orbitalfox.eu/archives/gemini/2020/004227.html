The users in the IRC channel don't feel comfortable having their
messages singled out and responded to in a different medium. The
consensus seems to be mailing list discussion should stay on the ML and
IRC discussion should stay in IRC. In either case, if they're not
comfortable with you doing it, it's probably a good idea to stop. It's
considered "rude" because it would be like taking a personal argument
and loudly responding in an airport.
