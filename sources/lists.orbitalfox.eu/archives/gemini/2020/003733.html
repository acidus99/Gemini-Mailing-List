Not a lot of content on my capsule but I've made a mirror
(automatically kept up-to-date) of the RFCs
<https://www.rfc-editor.org/>. Not only they are very important texts
but, since they have a plain text version, they fit well in the
geminispace. There is no text/gemini index yet, you
have to make the URL
'gemini://gemini.bortzmeyer.org/rfc-mirror/rfcNNNN.txt'. Two examples:

gemini://gemini.bortzmeyer.org/rfc-mirror/rfc8947.txt RFC 8947 (on MAC
addresses assignment through DHCP, published a few hours ago) from its
number

gemini://gemini.bortzmeyer.org/rfc-mirror/bcp/bcp47.txt BCP 47 (Best
Current Practice) about language tags, a pointer to the relevant RFC,
which is used in Gemini's specification.
