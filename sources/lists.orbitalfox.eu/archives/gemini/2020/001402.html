Hi everyone,

I'm pleased to announce version 0.4.0 of my bollux client for gemini (and now gopher) space. You can see the full release notes at the repo[1], but the biggest difference is the gopher support. It's still pretty basic and it doesn't do any complicated heuristic that gopher apparently requires in clients. (I must say that writing the gopher support made me appreciate gemini so much more!)

Extra thanks to those who've packaged bollux, told me about issues, and helped with openssl command-lining. Yall know who you are :)

As always, comments/concerns/bug reports/feature requests are welcome. Open an issue on the mailing list[2], @ me on fedi[3], or get in touch somehow else. Thanks for being a great community!
