Hi Gemini folks!

I thought I might introduce myself. My name is Adrian Cochrane, and I'm 
implementing an auditory web/gemini browser I call "Rhapsode". Rhapsode 
communicates purely through sound (and arrow keys for input) with no 
visual output!

My goal is to show the potential for a simpler JS-free web to work 
beautifully on any device, and when I was shown Gemini I liked the 
ideas and found it absolutely trivial to throw in support for that!

You can find Rhapsode's code at https://rhapsode.adrian.geek.nz/ , 
which is written primarily in Haskell.
