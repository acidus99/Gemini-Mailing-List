"A kill file (also killfile, bozo bin or twit list) is a file used by some ... reading programs to discard articles matching some unwanted patterns of subject, author, or other header lines. Adding a person or subject to one's kill file means that person or topic will be ignored by one's ... reader in the future. By extension, the term may be used for a decision to ignore the person or subject in other media."
