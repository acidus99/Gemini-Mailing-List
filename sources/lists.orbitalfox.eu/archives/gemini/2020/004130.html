I am announcing a new list of Gemini clients. My list is focused on
"full" clients, rather than libraries like go-gemini or tool like
gcat. As of this time I have all the clients listed on Gemini spec
software page, but I'm hoping that people will let me know about any
that I've missed.

As a companion to the list, I am also collecting client reviews.
Anyone is free to send in a review, please email the review to me
directly. Hopefully the combination of the list and reviews will make
for a good resource for finding new clients that fit your needs.

The list and reviews are both located at my capsule:
