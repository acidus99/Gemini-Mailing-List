I haven't used Usenet before but if there was an easy and costless way 
to use it (as there appears to be, based on the tutorial you linked) 
then I would certainly try it as an alternative to the mailing list. My 
initial thought was that there is no real reason to move away from the 
mailing list but it would actually be convenient to separate these 
discussions from my other emails (this is the only mailing list I use).

I suspect we would need to keep the mailing list and mirror it to Usenet 
though, as having Usenet as the only means of accessing community 
discussions would raise the barrier to entry to quite a high level IMO.
