Hey all,

The certificate for tilde.team has changed. Don't worry, we haven't been hacked; instead, we've now added our alternative domains (tild3.org, tilde.site, etc.) to Gemini! For instance, you can now access my userpage at tilde.team via gemini://fuckup.club/~khuxkm/! The cert is also now a long-term cert as opposed to the Let's Encrypt certs.

If your client doesn't support SAN, you may have issues connecting to tilde.team. I'm not sure what to tell you at this point, since both AV-98 and Amfora (both of the clients I use) accept domains in the SAN. However, you will continue to be able to access tilde.team even if your client doesn't support domains in the SAN.

Sincerely,
